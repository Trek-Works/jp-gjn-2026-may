🚗 Road Rules, Signs & Driving References
Emergency Numbers Japan
Emergency & Help · Know the key emergency numbers before driving: 110 for police and 119 for ambulance/fire.
View Sign
Hazards - Say Thank you
Courtesy & Hazards · Japanese drivers commonly flash hazard lights briefly as a thank-you after being let in or after courteous driving.
View Sign
Yellow Lines - Don’t Cross / Change Lanes
Lane Rules · Yellow lane lines generally mean do not cross or change lanes. Treat this as a strong restriction unless road markings clearly allow otherwise.
View Sign
Double Lines - White Side Changes Lanes
Lane Rules · Where double lines include a white side, lane changing may only be permitted from the white-line side, subject to signage and road conditions.
View Sign
Tolls - ETC Lanes
Tolls & ETC · Do not enter ETC-only toll lanes unless the vehicle has an ETC card/device fitted and active. Use general/cash/credit lanes if unsure.
View Sign
Parking - Back In
Parking · Back-in parking is common and often expected in Japan. Reverse into marked spaces where practical.
View Sign
Sign - No Parking
Road Signs · No parking. Do not park in the signed area or during the signed restriction period.
View Sign
Sign - No Stopping
Road Signs · No stopping. Do not stop or park in the signed area, even briefly, unless directed by police or traffic control.
View Sign
Sign - No Entry
Road Signs · Do not enter. Usually marks a one-way street or restricted-entry road.
View Sign
Sign - No Right Turn
Road Signs · Right turn prohibited at the signed intersection or location.
View Sign
Sign - Direction Only
Road Signs · Traffic must follow the indicated direction only.
View Sign
Sign - Stop
Road Signs · Come to a complete stop, check carefully, then proceed only when safe.
View Sign
Sign - Slowdown
Road Signs · Slow down and proceed with additional caution.
View Sign
Sign - Speed Limits
Road Signs · Observe posted speed limits. Local streets and expressways may vary significantly.
View Sign
Sign - Crosswalks
Road Signs · Watch for pedestrians and be prepared to stop at crossings.
View Sign
Sign - Parking
Parking · Parking permitted where signed, subject to any time or condition restrictions.
View Sign
Sign - Parking 60 mins between 8am - 8pm
Parking · Parking is limited to 60 minutes during the signed period. Check whether payment or display rules apply.
View Sign
Railway Crossings
Railway Crossings · Stop, check both directions, and proceed only when safe at railway crossings.
View Sign
Marked Drivers - Caution
Road Signs · Take extra care around marked vehicles and drivers who may be learners, elderly, or otherwise flagged for caution.
View Sign
Lights - Steady yellow prepare to stop
Traffic Lights · Prepare to stop when safe. Do not accelerate through yellow lights.
View Sign
Lights - RH turn on green arrow only
Traffic Lights · Right turns may only be permitted when the green arrow is displayed.
View Sign
Lights - Flashing yellow caution
Traffic Lights · Proceed with caution and check for pedestrians, cyclists, and cross traffic.
View Sign
Lights - Flashing red stop then go
Traffic Lights · Stop first, then proceed only when safe.
